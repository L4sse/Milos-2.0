About Miloš Mitrović

With over 10 years of experience as a trainer in water and polo sports, I bring expertise from Maribor and Graz. I combine functional training with manual therapy for a holistic approach. My style is calm and professional: We release blockages, improve posture, and build sustainable strength – far from bodybuilding hype. Let's bring your body into balance together.

My Philosophy & Approach

  • The body as a connected system: Everything is linked – from posture to mobility.
  • Functional movements first: Instead of isolation, we train real patterns for everyday life.
  • Release blockages, then build up: Hands-On work before strength training for safe progress.
  • Individual analysis: Every plan starts with your personal assessment and goals.
